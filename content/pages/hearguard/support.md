      ### Frequently Asked Questions


      **How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**

      Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then tap
      Subscriptions. Select the app and choose Cancel Subscription. You can
      also open the App Store app, tap your profile photo, and tap
      Subscriptions.


      **How do I restore my purchases?**

      Open the app, go to its Settings screen, and tap Restore Purchases.
      Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the
      original purchase.


      **How do I request a refund?**

      Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit
      reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the
      purchase you would like refunded.
