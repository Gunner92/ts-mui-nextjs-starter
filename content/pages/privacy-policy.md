      This Privacy Policy describes how BG Mobile Dev ("we", "us", or "our")
      collects, uses, and shares information when you use any of our mobile
      applications (collectively, the "Apps"), including but not limited to:
      Jet Lag Manager, Right Food - Toxic Scan, GlowUp, StressWatch,
      Mindful Minutes, Majority Goals, Bird Identifier, Animal Identifier,
      Signature Maker, Coin Identifier, Stamp Identifier, and Fossil Identifier.


      ## Information We Collect


      ### Information You Provide

      We may collect information you voluntarily provide when using our Apps,
      such as preferences, settings, and feedback. We do not require you to
      create an account to use our Apps.


      ### Automatically Collected Information

      When you use our Apps, we may automatically collect certain information,
      including:

      - Device type and operating system version

      - App usage metrics and interaction data

      - Crash reports and performance data

      - General location information (country/region level only)


      ### Health Data (StressWatch Only)

      StressWatch accesses Apple HealthKit data (heart rate, HRV) solely on
      your device for stress monitoring purposes. This health data is never
      transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.


      ### Camera and Photo Access

      Some of our Apps (Right Food, Bird Identifier, Animal Identifier,
      GlowUp, Coin Identifier, Stamp Identifier, Fossil Identifier) require
      camera or photo library access to provide their core functionality
      (scanning, identification). Images are processed either on-device or
      through secure AI services and are not stored on our servers after
      processing.


      ## How We Use Your Information


      We use the collected information to:

      - Provide and improve our Apps' functionality

      - Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

      - Fix bugs and improve performance

      - Develop new features based on usage trends


      We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third
      parties for their commercial purposes.


      ## Third-Party Services


      Our Apps may use the following third-party services:

      - **Apple App Store** for purchases and subscriptions

      - **RevenueCat** for subscription management

      - **Analytics services** for anonymous usage statistics

      - **AI processing services** for image recognition features


      These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of
      your information.


      ## Subscriptions


      Some of our Apps offer subscription-based features. Subscriptions are
      managed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled
      entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us. Subscriptions
      automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end
      of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your
      App Store account settings.


      ## Data Retention


      We retain automatically collected information for as long as necessary
      to provide our services and for analytical purposes. When we no longer
      need the information, we either delete it or anonymize it so that you
      cannot be identified.


      ## Data Security


      We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to
      protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration,
      disclosure, or destruction.


      ## Children's Privacy


      Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not
      knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we
      learn that we have collected personal information from a child under
      13, we will take steps to delete such information.


      ## Your Rights


      Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

      - Access the personal information we hold about you

      - Request correction of inaccurate information

      - Request deletion of your information

      - Object to or restrict processing of your information

      - Data portability


      ## Changes to This Privacy Policy


      We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify
      you of any significant changes through our Apps or other means. Your
      continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the
      updated policy.
